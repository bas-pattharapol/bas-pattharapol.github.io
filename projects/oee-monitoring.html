OEE Monitoring System

UNILEVER THAI HOLDING (Minburi)

Developed a comprehensive production monitoring and loss analysis platform that enables real-time tracking of Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE), helping manufacturing teams identify and reduce losses effectively.

Project Overview

The OEE Monitoring System was designed to provide UNILEVER's Minburi facility with real-time visibility into their production performance. The system tracks and analyzes the three key components of OEE: Availability, Performance, and Quality.
